New here? The 30-second tour

Describe an electronic device in plain English. A team of specialist AI agents turns it into an engineering-ready KiCad project — the same files a hardware engineer would hand to a PCB designer.

  • No electronics knowledge needed — just describe the idea, or load an example below.
  • A team, not one model: an Architect proposes, a Critic challenges it, a Chief Engineer resolves the conflicts — then PCB specialists prepare the board.
  • You get a live block diagram, a PCB-readiness pack, a PDF report, and downloadable KiCad files.
  • Set your Audience (Professional / Student / Maker) and every explanation adapts to you.

📊 Why a team? In a 5-design benchmark the agents surfaced 11.6 / 12 engineering concerns on average vs 8.4 / 12 for a single AI (+3.2). Reproduce it under “Advanced · compare & benchmark”.
